OUR SERVICES

EMERGENCY TREATMENT

  • Tooth Sedation

    • Tooth sedation, or dental sedation, refers to the use of sedative medications to help patients relax or reduce anxiety during dental procedures. Sedation is commonly used in dentistry to create a more comfortable and stress-free experience, particularly for individuals who may have dental phobia or anxiety.

    In emergency dental situations, such as severe tooth pain, trauma, or infections, providing prompt and effective care is crucial. Sedation may be considered to manage pain, reduce anxiety, and facilitate necessary emergency treatments.

  • Tooth Reimplantation

    • Tooth reimplantation, also known as dental replantation, is a dental procedure where a previously avulsed (knocked-out) tooth is reinserted into its socket in the jawbone. This is typically done after a traumatic injury or accident has caused the tooth to be completely knocked out of its position.

    Tooth reimplantation is a time-sensitive and delicate procedure that requires immediate attention and professional dental care.

  • Incision and Drainage (I&D)

    • Incision and Drainage (I&D) is a medical procedure performed to treat localized infections or abscesses by creating an incision (a small cut) to allow the drainage of pus and infected material. This procedure is commonly used to relieve pain, promote healing, and prevent the spread of infection to surrounding tissues. Incision and Drainage is a common and effective procedure for managing localized infections and abscesses.

    Emergency incision and drainage are crucial for addressing acute dental infections promptly, providing relief from pain, and preventing the spread of infection to other parts of the body. Seeking emergency dental care ensures timely intervention and appropriate management of dental emergencies.

    Aside from these treatments, we offer a wide range of treatment for any dental healthcare emergencies and to help alleviate any pain and to restore oral health. Whether you require immediate attention for a dental emergency, preventive services to maintain optimal oral health, or specialized treatments to address specific dental issues, we are here to meet your needs.
